Who Am I?

I'm a person who loves snow. Do you know that your favourite season could reveal your personality? Donning a warm sweater and curling up on the couch with a hot beverage to escape the cold might sound like my idea of an ideal afternoon.

Chai is not just a beverage, chai is love. Chai is an emotion, to be fair, chai is every emotion. Especially, Adrak Chai (Ginger tea) is my all time favourite. Who needs a guy when there's chai...Life is just like a cup of tea. It's all in how you make it.

It was love at first sight the day I met the beach. I have always want to be wild, beautiful and free Just like the sea. An ocean breeze puts my mind at ease. If there's a heaven for me, I'm sure it has a beach attached to it. Sky above,sand below,peace within.
